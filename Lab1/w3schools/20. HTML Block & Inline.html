About Eeveee

Eevee is a unique pokemon. It is very loyal and has 8 evolved forms: Glaceon, Umbreon, Leafeon, Flareon, Jolteon, Sylveon, Espion, Vaporeon.

My favourite eeveelution is Sylveon: they are cute, they are tricky, and they are obtained only when an eevee loves their trainer a lot.
